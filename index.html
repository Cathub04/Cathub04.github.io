About Airliners


An Airliner is what we usually called planes but used by airlines to transport travellers.

You might have already travelled with them many times, and notice that they looks similar but still have some differences on size and detailed designs

For common models we can see in the airport, they are mainly from two manufacturer, one is Boeing, the other is Airbus.


Click the logo to visit their official website

Two of the most common model you can see are the Boeing 787 Dreamliner series(left) and the Airbus A330 series(right).
